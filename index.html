About Us

Ceracem Enterprises has been a trusted name in refractory solutions for over 40 years. What began as a dedicated supplier of high-quality firebricks has grown into a reliable partner for businesses across India and beyond.

With strong, long-standing relationships with top manufacturers, we offer competitive pricing and maintain strategically located warehouses to ensure timely and efficient deliveries throughout the country.

We specialize in a comprehensive range of shaped and unshaped refractory materials designed to withstand extreme temperatures and meet the demanding needs of various industries. Our product portfolio includes high-performance firebricks, cement castables, and other precision-engineered solutions ideal for critical high-heat environments.
